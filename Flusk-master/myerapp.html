MyERApp: Simplifying Emergency Dental Care

MyERApp solves the common problem of finding immediate dental help. Patients can easily find nearby dentists, book appointments (chat, video, or in-person), and make secure payments. Doctors manage their availability, set rates, and access patient information securely. A per-consultation fee model ensures fair compensation for doctors while maintaining affordability for patients.
