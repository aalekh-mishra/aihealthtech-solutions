Beyond Cosmetics: The Revolutionary Power of Medical Orthodontics

The American Academy of Medical Orthodontics® (AAMO) is leading the way in a revolutionary approach to healthcare. We utilize advanced orthodontic techniques to treat dozens of chronic medical and dental conditions, often eliminating the need for surgery or long-term medication. By addressing the underlying craniofacial imbalances, we achieve holistic health improvements, enhancing patient well-being and reducing healthcare costs.

Medical Orthodontics: Addressing a Spectrum of Conditions

Medical Orthodontics offers non-invasive solutions for a wide array of issues, including:

  • Medical Issues: Migraines, headaches, tinnitus, sleep apnea, insomnia, ADD, posture problems, neck and shoulder pain, back pain, digestive issues, TMJ disorders, and more.
  • Dental Issues: Periodontal disease, halitosis, alveolar bone loss, gum recession, teeth sensitivity, grinding, clenching, and more.
  • Cosmetic Issues: Facial asymmetry, gummy smiles, wrinkles, and midline shifts.
